Parametric Shelf System

An adaptive shelving concept exploring modular design principles and digital fabrication.

The Parametric Shelf System explores how algorithmic and parametric design can support adaptable and customizable furniture. Using digital fabrication tools, the shelf’s structure can be modified to fit different spaces and user needs — merging craftsmanship with generative design.
