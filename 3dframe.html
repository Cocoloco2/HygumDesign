Feel You Pictures

A tactile photography experiment blending visual media with sensory interaction.

Feel You Pictures is an exploration of how photography can be experienced beyond the visual sense. By combining tactile surfaces and embedded sensors, the project transforms static images into interactive objects — allowing viewers to “feel” the photograph through touch-based feedback and sound.
